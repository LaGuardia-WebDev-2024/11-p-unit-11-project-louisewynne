My Top 3 Memories of This Year

Boston Day Trip
Halloween
Bowling
In March, five of my friends and I took a bus to Boston for a day. We walked around the city, visiting places like the Boston Museum of Fine Arts, the Boston Public Library, Faneuil Hall marketplace (where we got food), and the many statues and buildings. We nearly missed our bus back to NYC and had to sprint to catch it.
Me and some of my friends spent last Halloween together and it was really fun walking around seeing people in costumes and eating candy. We also went into a haunted house and it was so scary I genuinely thought I was gonna die.
In April me and two of my friends spent the day getting food in Flushing and then going bowling. I sucked and lost every round but it was still super fun.
